I am a graduate student at the GRAB Lab at Yale University. I work on robotic manipulation, specifically how hand design can be leveraged to increase dexterity. I have a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln (B.S. 2014) and two Master's degrees from Yale (M.S. & M.Phil 2018). In the past, I have worked at Tethon3D (2015), the NASA Jet Propulsion Lab (2014), and Honeybee Robotics (2013).
